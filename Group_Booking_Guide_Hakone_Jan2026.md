- **Always confirm specific policy with each hotel**

#### âš ï¸ Payment Terms (Typical)
- Deposit: 20-30% upon booking confirmation
- Balance: 14-30 days before arrival
- Methods: Bank transfer, credit card, PayPal (varies by hotel)
- Foreign currency: Most hotels accept JPY only, some accept USD/KRW

#### âš ï¸ Group Booking Benefits
